Who We Are

Luciane Costa

Luciane Mallmann Costa is a lawyer with 15 years of experience, specializing in advising clients on UK and EU Immigration Law, Human Rights, and UK Family Law.

Luciane has extensive experience in complex matters involving UK Immigration Law; EU free movement and permanent residence in the UK for EEA nationals and their non-EEA family members, involving complex applications based on EU jurisprudence; applications for indefinite leave to remain in the UK; naturalization; and registration of minors as British citizens. Luciane is also engaged in sensitive human rights cases and dedicates a significant portion of her human rights practice to pro bono matters. In addition, she is instructed by clients in a broad range of family law issues including divorce and child matters.

Pietro Costa

Pietro Costa is an associate based in the London office of LMC and is a member of the Corporate Group, with a particular focus on employment law aspects of transactional matters.

Pietro advises domestic and international clients on a broad range of both corporate and employment matters across a variety of sectors, including technology, retail, financial services and energy. Pietro has worked with clients ranging from large international corporates and financial institutions to emerging companies, particularly in the technology and media industries.

On employment matters, Pietro provides advice on labour law, employee benefits and executive compensation, litigation and internal investigations. Pietro also advises on a range of corporate transactions including mergers and acquisitions, corporate restructurings and venture capital investments.
